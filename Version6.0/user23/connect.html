Meet our Teen Psychologists

Bio

Emily is a Licensed Professional Clinical Counselor and Certified Clinical Trauma Professional. She provides individual therapy for adults and adolescent clients ages 16 and up.

Hlee enjoys working with adolescents ages 13+, young adults, and adults. She specializes in supporting individuals with anxiety, depression, trauma, suicidal ideation/self-harm, life transitions, and identity and cultural concerns.

Michael specializes in men’s issues, adolescent male teens (16+), and grieving fathers. he emphasizes safety, curiosity, and humor when he collaborate with his clients.

Are you an older teen or young adult on your own for the first time (or soon to be!), and feeling overwhelmed? You are far from alone! Sara will listen carefully to your concerns and help you gain insight, clarity, and skills to allow you to feel more empowered and confident during this important transition.

Richard's sessions involve assisting individuals and families to address concerns including: Shame, grief, loss, substance abuse, anger, life transitions (i.e. teen years to adulthood), PTSD, trauma and dissociation, ADHD, anxiety, stress, mood disorders, depression, parenting, the unique challenges that many immigrants face, and cross-cultural/multi-ethnic couples and families.
