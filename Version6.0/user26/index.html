About

Rider Hub Deluxe was an idea concocted in the amazing parking lot of Shakopee High School in Shakopee, Minnesota. In order to pay for kids' dreams and help the world, we needed an idea to generate money. We figured rather than going to every dealership separately and finding the cars for you as they pester you trying to get you to buy the most expensive car, you can look at the available stock at every car brand. Our services are completely free and allow you to access each brand's website if you're interested. We're everything all in one place.
